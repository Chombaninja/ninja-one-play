Number of Players: Killer is most fun with three or more players.

Play: A player throws one dart with the "opposite" hand. (right-handers throw left-handed) to determine randomly his or her own number(1-numbers for 3-lifes or 3-numbers for 1-life each). If a player misses the dartboard or hits a number already taken, he or she throws again.

Rules of the Game: Each player first hit the double of his or her own number. When this is acheived, the player is known as a "killer" and a K is placed after his name on the scoreboard.After becoming a killer, a player begins aiming for the doubles of opponents' numbers. If a killer hits his or her own double by mistake, the killer loses one live. To kill an opponent fast hit three doubles.
